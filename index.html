Papa Drag

Papa Drag is a French figure who first emerged in the national media for his powerful message around identity, self-acceptance, and the beauty of difference. Choosing artistic integrity over constant visibility, he stepped back from the spotlight to follow his own creative path.

Now based in Berlin, he returns with a new project rooted in transformation. Known for his powerful and distinctive voice, Papa Drag brings a rare intensity to the stage. Through his reinterpretations, familiar songs are reborn with emotional depth and a renewed sense of purpose. A new chapter begins — bold, free, and resolutely his own.
